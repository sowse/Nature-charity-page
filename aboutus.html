About us

Nature's Vigil is an organization committed towards protecting nature.

For 30 years, we have combined volunteer action, lobbying, and awareness campaigns in order to achieve our goals.

With our global network, and your help, we have managed to protect and preserve key areas in places of environmental interest.

 

Our goals

Wildlife Preservation

We aim to preserve wildlife populations in several areas. We do this by petitioning key figures in order to push through change and measures that protect several endangered species.

Reforestation Efforts

We take action towards planting new life in areas that have been ravaged by forest fires. Deforestation poses a constant risk for soil erosion that we strive to minimize.

Clean-up Drives

We organize volunteer action with the goal of cleaning up areas of environmental interest. Our goals is to preserve these areas for everyone to enjoy and appreciate.
